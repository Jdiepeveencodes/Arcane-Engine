## üìñ Story Writing Tips

### Background Story (Required)
Answer these questions:
- Where were you born?
- What was your childhood like?
- What triggered your adventure?
- What's your biggest regret?
- What are your goals?

**Template**: "I was born in [place]. My childhood was [description]. When [major event], I decided to [action]. Now I adventure to [goal]."

**Minimum**: 50 words (2-3 sentences)  
**Better**: 100+ words (3-4 paragraphs)

### Personality Traits
Pick 2-3 that define you:
- Speech patterns ("I speak in riddles")
- Habits ("Always tap fingers when thinking")
- Beliefs ("Help those in need")
- Fears ("Terrified of fire")

### Ideals (What You Fight For)
- **Justice**: Make the world fairer
- **Loyalty**: Protect your friends
- **Knowledge**: Understand everything
- **Freedom**: Liberate the oppressed
- **Ambition**: Become powerful/famous
- **Compassion**: Help the helpless

### Bonds (Personal Connections)
Link to:
- Other players' characters
- Important NPCs
- Your mission/goal
- Your past

Example: "I owe [player] my life" or "I seek my missing sibling"

### Flaws (Make You Interesting)
- "Gamble away gold recklessly"
- "Quick to anger, act before thinking"
- "Trust easily, often betrayed"
- "Terrified of magic"
- "Compulsively lie"

## üî¢ Rolling Ability Scores (Optional)

If your DM allows rolling instead of standard array:

1. Roll 4d6 (four six-sided dice)
2. Drop the lowest die
3. Add the 3 remaining dice
4. Write down the total
5. Repeat 6 times (once for each ability)
6. Arrange the 6 totals however you want

Example rolls: 15, 14, 13, 12, 10, 8 (congratulations!)
